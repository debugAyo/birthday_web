soooo this started as a birthday present for my mom, but somewhere along the way it turned into something else. a love letter, basically. a whole interactive webpage for someone special.

it's got that whole thing where you scroll down and stuff happens — a countdown, an envelope you click open with a poem inside, a handwritten-looking letter with two more poems, a little gallery of memories, and then confetti + balloons + a typewriter message at the end. the whole nine yards.

built it with just html, css, and javascript. no frameworks, no libraries, nothing fancy. just raw code and way too much time tweaking animations.

you can change literally everything — the name, the birthday date, what the letter says, the poems, the memory cards, the colors. it's all marked with ✦ CHANGE: comments so you don't have to hunt for anything.

if you want to use it for someone, just swap the text and images. or don't. it works as-is too.

made with love, obviously.
